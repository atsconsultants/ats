Assist to Succeed Consulting

CatholicCare Diocese of Wollongong has had the privilege of working with ATS as a trusted partner for over a decade. The mission of ATS is encapsulated in their name - assisting not for profit community organisations to succeed.

The ATS team are highly experienced professionals from diverse corporate backgrounds inspired by a shared commitment to bringing this mission to life in the community.  Such is their commitment that all the services provided by ATS are generously offered on a strict pro bono basis, while operating with all the professionalism of a private consultancy firm.

We have found the approach of the ATS team to be highly ethical and mission aligned.  They invite fresh thinking and approaches around strategy, offer project management guidance and coaching, help improve discipline with strategy execution, and at times take the role of a trusted 'critical friend' to help us stretch and grow our vision of what is possible.  Throughout their engagement with us on a different projects, we have found them to be consistently respectful in approach, and always seeking opportunities for mutual learning to take place.

The partnership with ATS has been invaluable, and we look forward very much to continuing our relationship with them into the future.

Michael Austin
CEO
CatholiCare Diocese of Wollongong
January 2025
